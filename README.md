# Adidas Performance Hub - Interactive Sales Dashboard

<img src="adidas-dashboard.png" width="100%">

## 📊 Project Overview
An interactive and dynamic Excel dashboard designed to analyze Adidas sales performance, revenue trends, and profitability. This project transforms raw retail data into actionable business insights to optimize sales strategies, monitor regional market shares, and track product category performance.

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights
* **Top Market:** **Egypt** dominates the market share with **30%** of total revenue, while **Lebanon** represents the smallest market at **6%**.
* **Product Performance:** **Footwear** is the primary revenue driver, accounting for **62%** of total sales. 
* **Gender & Segment Dominance:** **Men's Footwear** is the top-performing segment across all categories, capturing **35.26%** of total sales.
* **Profitability Champions:** **Predator Freak** leads the top 5 profitable products with the highest net profit ($9,218).
* **Sales Channels:** **Online sales** dominate the distribution channels with a **46%** share.

---

## 🛠️ Features & Tools Used
* **Data Cleansing & Transformation:** Organized raw data and structured fields for analysis.
* **Pivot Tables & Calculated Fields:** Generated dynamic summaries and custom metrics like **Profit Margin %**.
* **Advanced Interactive Charts:** Built clustered bar charts, combo charts (secondary axis for trends), and doughnut charts.
* **Dynamic Slicers:** Integrated interactive filters for *Category, Region, Store Type, and Order Date* to enable seamless data exploration.
